Experience & Efficiency


X-Live Production stands at the forefront of creative production in the Middle East, delivering world-class branding, signage, and structural installations for some of the region’s most prestigious events.

With an integrated team of designers, engineers, fabricators, and project managers, we ensure seamless coordination, rapid execution, and uncompromising quality—bringing ambitious concepts to life with precision and scale.

Core Expertise

Technical Production

We provide end-to-end production solutions including stage design and fabrication, advanced lighting design and programming, professional audio and sound systems, high-resolution LED screens and media façades, immersive projection mapping, and a full range of special effects such as pyrotechnics, CO₂, confetti, and laser shows.

Event Build & Fabrication

Our capabilities include the design and execution of integrated branding and signage systems, bespoke structural builds and scenic elements, high-impact exhibition stands and pavilions, and both temporary and semi-permanent installations, supported by sustainable and modular construction methods that enable adaptability, scalability, and efficient deployment across diverse environments.

Live Show Production

From grand opening and closing ceremonies to dynamic live shows and performances, we orchestrate every element with precision—handling artist and talent coordination, real-time show calling and cue control, and detailed rehearsals and technical run-throughs to deliver seamless, high-impact productions.

Event Operations

We provide end-to-end project management supported by detailed production scheduling and logistics planning, seamless vendor and supplier coordination, comprehensive health, safety, and risk management, and dedicated on-ground event supervision to ensure every project is delivered efficiently, safely, and to the highest standards.

Digital Experiences

Our capabilities extend into next-generation experiences through virtual and hybrid events, incorporating augmented and virtual reality activations, interactive installations, and high-quality virtual production and content creation, enabling brands to connect with audiences in dynamic, immersive, and digitally enhanced environments.

Media Production

From dynamic event photography and cinematic videography to refined post-production and high-impact digital screen content, we create compelling visual experiences that capture attention, tell stories, and elevate brand presence at every touchpoint.
